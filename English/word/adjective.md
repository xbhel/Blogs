### 常见形容词

|        原级        |     比较级      |      最高级      |
| :----------------: | :-------------: | :--------------: |
| expensive (昂贵的) | more expensive  |  most expensive  |
| beautiful (漂亮的) | more beautiful  |  most beautiful  |
| gorgeous (迷人的)  |  more gorgeous  |  most gorgeous   |
|    hot (性感的)    |     hotter      |     hottest      |
|    high (高的)     | higher (更高的) | highest (最高的) |
